Best of Babson

The Best of Babson is a celebration of the people and organizations that help make Babson a wonderful place to live and learn. Join us in honoring the Best of Babson at our awards ceremony on April 14th, 2015.


Organization of the Year

This student group worked collaboratively with other groups (organizations or Babson divisions) on campus, made an impact in Babson's community, strengthened its membership and membership opportunities, and did something new and innovative this year.


Unsung Hero

This student, faculty, or staff member was constantly behind the scenes, pulling last-minute details together and supporting programs and student group(s), without recognition. This individual deserves to be thanked for all of his or her hard work.


Ted Grossman Beaver Believer

This student, faculty, or staff member is universally recognized for his/her passion for students, support of student leadership development, and pride in Babson as a whole. Students must be in good standing with Babson College in order to receive this award.


Organization with the Most Outstanding Programming

This student group hosted the best event(s) on campus in the 2013-2014 academic year.


Sustainability Leadership Award

This student leader has demonstrated a commitment to being a sustainability leader at Babson, and may show leadership in terms of environmental, cultural, social, financial, and personal sustainability practices and principles.


Student Business of the Year

Whether it achieved amazing sales, offered incredible value to the community, upheld sustainability values, exemplified Entrepreneurial Thought and Action, or showed overall creativity of a product/service, this student business really stood out among the others.


Advisor of the Year

This faculty or staff member made a significant contribution to his or her advisee group this year by providing mentoring, support, and encouragement related to event planning, brainstorming, marketing, etc.


Club Sports Team of the Year

This club sport team demonstrated outstanding sportsmanship to officials/reps and opposing teams and represents Babson with integrity and honor.
