Best of Babson

The Best of Babson is a celebration of the people and organizations that help make Babson a wonderful place to live and learn. Join us in honoring the Best of Babson at our awards ceremony on April 14th, 2015.


Student Leader of the Year

This student has made an outstanding contribution to the Babson experience through their leadership. This student has empowered their team to achieve great success. This student uses their influence for the greater good, making ethical decisions that make the campus a better place.

Ted Grossman Beaver Believer Award

This student, faculty, or staff member is universally recognized for their passion for students, support of student leadership development, and pride in Babson as a whole.

Club or Organization of the Year

This student group worked collaboratively with other groups (organizations or Babson divisions) on campus, made an impact in Babson's community, strengthened its membership and membership opportunities, and did something new and innovative this year.

Student Business of the Year

Whether it offered great value to the community, upheld sustainability values, exemplified Entrepreneurial Thought and Action, or showed overall creativity of a product/service, this student business really stood out among the others.

Advisor of the Year

This faculty or staff member made a significant contribution to their advisee group this year by providing mentoring, support, and encouragement related to event planning, brainstorming, marketing for an organization or club.

Faculty of the Year

This member of the teaching faculty has made outstanding contributions to the educational experience at Babson. They go above and beyond the call of duty to cultivate a learning environment in the classroom and the spark the curiosity of their students.

Student Athlete of the Year

This student has not only excelled in their sport, but has also shown to be a great teammate. The student is an outstanding member of the community, who demonstrates hard work and commitment to the sport while embodying Babson’s values on and off the field.

FME Company of the Year

The company exhibits the true fulfillment of innovation and corroboration on the student level. An award winning company is a stellar example of the Entrepreneurial Thought & Action methodology taught in the FME course. The company not only created a feasible product/service, but also an original one that made an impact both socially and economically.

Student Artist of the Year

This student embodies both creativity and innovation, that have long been hallmarks of Babson College’s focus on entrepreneurship. The student fulfilled these values by engaging in activities involving theater, visual arts, film, music or even dance. Most importantly, the student is able to reflect their passion to the student body when participating in these activities.

Campus Involvement Leader of the Year

This student has the entrepreneurial mindset that Babson promotes by being involved in various organizations. The student both attends and promotes a lot of events. Overall, the student is very present on campus and puts a smile on everyone’s face.
