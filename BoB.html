Best of Babson

The Best of Babson is a celebration of the people and organizations that help make Babson a wonderful place to live and learn. Join us in honoring the Best of Babson at our awards ceremony on April 14th, 2015.

Student Leader of the Year

This student has made an outstanding contribution to the Babson experience through their leadership and involvement with clubs/organizations. This student has empowered their team to achieve great success and uses their influence for the greater good, making ethical decisions that make Babson a better place.


Club Sports/Intramural Student-Athelete of the Year

This student has not only excelled in their sport, but has also shown to be a great teammate and scholar. The student is an outstanding member of the community, who demonstrates hard work and commitment to the sport while embodying Babson’s values on and off the field. I’m not sure what to have for the description until we solidify who is eligible. If it’s only club sports/intramurals, then I think the description needs to change.


Ted Grossman Beaver Believer Award

This student, faculty, or staff member is universally recognized for their passion for the Babson community, their support of leadership development, and pride in Babson as a whole. If a student is nominated, they must be in good standing with Babson College in order to receive this award.


Organization of the Year

This student group worked collaboratively with other groups (organizations or Babson departments) on campus, made an impact in Babson's community, strengthened its membership and membership opportunities, and did something new and innovative this year.


Event of the Year

This event was well planned and executed by the organizing group, and was well attended by the Babson community. The group that organized the event connected with the appropriate campus resources, planned in a timely manner, and had good attendance due to quality marketing. This event was unique, innovative, and/or added value to the campus.


Best Collaboration

An event or initiative was strengthened because two or more student organizations worked together toward their effort. This could also apply to a student group that partnered with a campus department, staff, or faculty member. This collaboration led to increased awareness, attendance, and/or school spirit.


Student Business of the Year

Whether it offered great value to the community, upheld sustainability values, exemplified Entrepreneurial Thought and Action, or showed overall creativity of a product or service, this student business really stood out among the others.


Outstanding Community Service

This award goes to the student organization that showed a true dedication to serving the greater community, whether through the mission of their organization, or through the efforts of the individual members. This award does not need to go to a service-oriented organization, but should go to an organization that donated their time, talent, or goods to those in need.


Organization Advisor of the Year

This faculty or staff member made a significant contribution to their advisee group this year by providing mentoring, support, and encouragement related to event planning, brainstorming, and marketing. They attended meetings and/or events held by the group, and promoted learning through the group’s co-curricular involvement.


Faculty of the Year

This member of the teaching faculty has made outstanding contributions to the educational experience at Babson. They go above and beyond the call of duty to cultivate a learning environment in the classroom and the spark the curiosity of their students.


Student Artist of the Year

This student embodies both creativity and innovation, that have long been hallmarks of Babson College’s focus on entrepreneurship. The student fulfilled these values by engaging in activities involving theater, visual arts, film, music or dance. The student is also able to reflect their passion for the arts to the student body when participating in these activities and helps to increase overall interest in the arts.


FME Company of the Year

The company exhibits the true fulfillment of innovation and collaboration. An award winning company is a stellar example of the Entrepreneurial Thought & Action methodology taught in the FME course. The company not only created a feasible product or service, but also an original one that made an impact both socially and economically. This group also worked well with campus partners, and used their resources wisely to increase their chances for success.


Campus Involvement Leader of the Year

This student has the entrepreneurial mindset that Babson promotes by being involved in various organizations. The student both attends and promotes a lot of events. Overall, the student is very present on campus and puts a smile on everyone’s face.
